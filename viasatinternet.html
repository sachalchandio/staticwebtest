You initiate a website request on your laptop or mobile device, which is transmitted to your Viasat modem and then to the dish outside your home. The dish at your home transmits your request to Viasat's satellite. The satellite relays your website request to a gateway station connected to Viasat's ground network. The station forwards your request to the desired website, which sends the requested information back to the station. The station then transmits it back up to the satellite, which delivers it directly to you.
